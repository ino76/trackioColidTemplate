Variants

Our solution is very customizable so you can choose an option that suits your needs.

Wi-Fi Sensors

  • We are able to track customers through their mobile phones and identify returning visitors.

Intelligent Cameras

  • Using both machine learning and face recognition algorithms our cameras are able to track customers and provide details about them.

Digital Signage

  • Plug & Play solution. Smart advertising LCD panel allows to customize content by both age and gender.
