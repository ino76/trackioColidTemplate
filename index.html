More about sensors ...

The system usually consists of multiple short range sensors:

  • standard dimension is 15x15x3cm & does not have to be visible
  • powered by Power over Ethernet, up to 100m from the switch
  • visitors position accuracy with 1 sensor per 20m is 1 - 3m
  • we are able to track 75 - 85% of visitors

Additionally you can integrate your transactional data ...

to our platform to have insight about conversions of different parts of your store. The cloud platform also offers data using our API.
