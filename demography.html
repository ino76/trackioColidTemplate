DEMOGRAPHY MODULE

If your product has its specific audience then demographic data of people moving in the vicinity of your shop can be very valuable for you.

#1

Gender

Our face recognition algorithm is able to determine whether the person in the vicinity of the camera is male or female. This functionality is extremely helpful when you target products at a particular gender.

#2

Age Groups

Face recognition algorithm helps us to classify customers into 6 age groups:

  • 18 - 24
  • 25 - 34
  • 35 - 44
  • 45 - 54
  • 55 - 65
  • 65+
