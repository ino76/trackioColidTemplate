#1: Revenue of Customer Retention

Let's have several visitors: blondie, redhead, brunette, some guy and afro american woman. As store manager you want to know who of them is in your store for the first time and who of them is regular customer. With knowledge about customer retention you can compare monthly revenues and get an idea about your campaigns efficiency.

January

Blondie, redhead and brunette came into store. They bought some stuff. Blondie spent 1000$, redhead 2300$ and brunette 500$. January Revenue is 3800$.

March

Blondie came also in March and spent another 500$, so overall revenue of her is 1500$ now. Redhead came in March too however she did not spend any money. Plus two new customers came in March: guy with brown hair who spent 5000$ and afro american woman who spent 900$.

March

Blondie came also in March and spent another 500$, so overall revenue of her is 1500$ now. Redhead came in March too however she did not spend any money. Plus two new customers came in March: guy with brown hair who spent 5000$ and afro american woman who spent 900$.
