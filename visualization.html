Heatmap

The movement around the store is recorded on the Heatmap which is shown above the simplified map of aÂ particular store. You can easily find out where the visitors are moving the most frequently and according to that, for example, you can move the shelves, display the goods on sale or allocate the employees.

Visits

The map Visits allows to easily check the movement of an individual at selected time and to see the duration of this movement.

Sections

The map Sections provides information about the individual sections of the store. Thanks to this part we can easily distinguish which sections of the store bring the highest profits and which are visited the most frequently.
