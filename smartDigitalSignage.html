SMART DIGITAL SIGNAGE

Smart Digital Signage is Plug & Play LCD panel with built-in camera and LTE internet. It allows to customize the advertising content by both age and gender. Everything is easily managed through a web application.

#1

Video Management

You can easily manage your videos through our web application. Select a target group, upload a video and see whether the conversion rate will match your expectations.

#2

Heatmap

The movement in front of the digital signage is recorded on the heatmap which is shown above the simplified map of a selected part of the store. You can easily find out where the visitors are moving the most frequently.

#3

Visitor

Situation: A person comes from the bottom, immediately enters the shop and after a certain amount of time exits the shop. Output: In the application, the person’s route is tracked and the person is counted as a visitor.

#4

Passed-by

Situation: A person passes by the shop without stopping by and looking at the digital signage in the shopping window. Output: In the application, the person’s route is tracked and the person is counted as a passer-by.

#5

Observer

Situation: A person goes by the shop, is attracted by the content on the display, stops by for a short while and leaves afterwards. Output: In the application, the person’s route is tracked and the person is counted as an attentive passer-by.

#6

Converted

Situation: A person goes by the shop, is attracted by the advertisement and enters the shop. Output: In the application, the person’s route is tracked and the person is counted as converted.
